About Me

Learn more about my background, skills, and what drives me in the field of cybersecurity and programming.

Who I Am

I'm a focused developer with a passion for building not just functional, but fundamentally secure software from the ground up. Growing up in Norway, my curiosity about how systems work and how they can be broken has driven me to constantly learn and improve. I currently live in Kongsberg, Norway.

My approach combines technical precision with creative problem-solving. I enjoy the challenge of thinking like an attacker to build better defenses, and I'm particularly passionate about making security accessible to other developers.

Professional Goals

My goal is to specialize in the field of cybersecurity. I focus specifically on vulnerability testing and penetration testing, in other words, ethical hacking. I'm committed to developing expertise that allows me to identify and address security vulnerabilities effectively while maintaining ethical standards in the cybersecurity industry.

Professional Background

I am a dedicated cybersecurity enthusiast with extensive programming experience in C#, Python, and PHP. My work focuses on developing secure applications and implementing robust security measures to protect digital assets. I continuously enhance my skills in penetration testing and vulnerability assessment, staying current with the latest security trends and threats.

With a strong technical foundation, I approach cybersecurity challenges methodically, combining analytical thinking with creative problem-solving to develop effective security solutions. I'm particularly interested in application security, secure coding practices, and building tools that enhance both security and productivity.

Work Experience

While I'm still a student and haven't had regular employment, I have participated in work placements organized by my school. In 2024, I completed a placement at Kongsberg Defence & Aerospace, a company with high security standards, which provided valuable insight into professional security practices in a high-stakes environment.
